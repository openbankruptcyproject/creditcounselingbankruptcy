Finding Approved Credit Counseling Agencies

Only agencies approved by the U.S. Trustee count - here is how to find them

Home›Approved Credit Counseling Agencies for Bankruptcy

Why "Approved" Matters

Under 11 U.S.C. Section 109(h), the credit counseling course required before filing bankruptcy must be provided by an agency approved by the Executive Office for United States Trustees (EOUST). A certificate from a non-approved agency will not be accepted by the court, and your case could be dismissed.

How to Find Approved Agencies

The U.S. Department of Justice maintains the official list at:

justice.gov/ust - Approved Credit Counseling Agencies

You must select an agency approved for the judicial district where you will file. Most agencies are approved nationwide, but some serve only specific districts.

What to Look For

  • Approved for your district: Confirm the agency is approved for your judicial district (e.g., Western District of Missouri, Northern District of Illinois)
  • Method of delivery: Most agencies offer online, phone, and in-person options. Online is typically the most convenient.
  • Cost: Typical range is $15-50. Fee waivers are available for those who cannot afford it.
  • Languages: Some agencies offer courses in Spanish and other languages
  • Certificate delivery: Make sure the agency will provide the certificate immediately upon completion - you need it to file

Popular Approved Agencies

  • GreenPath Financial Wellness
  • InCharge Debt Solutions
  • Money Management International
  • National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC) members
  • Consolidated Credit

This is not an endorsement of any specific agency. Always verify current approval status on the DOJ list before enrolling.
